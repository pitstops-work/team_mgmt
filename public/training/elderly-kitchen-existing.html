Monitoring & Tracking

Why this pitstop

Monthly monitoring of all 3 community kitchen(s). CO visits daily; RP visits all kitchens in the cluster monthly. Covers food quality, inventory, beneficiary coverage, vendor management, and substitute arrangements. Elderly kitchens are also a standing agenda item in the monthly cluster meeting.
