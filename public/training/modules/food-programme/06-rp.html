Module 6 · Full 4-week onboarding

Resource Person — Operator of Record

RP Our staff · 02:00 to 11:00 shift
The RP's standing

The RP is not an auditor. The RP is the operator-of-record. If the kitchen is late or a DP runs out, the RP is accountable for noticing, escalating, and writing down what we learn. The RP observes, documents, and escalates — and never handles money, hiring or firing of vendor staff, or vendor price.

6.2Coverage rules the RP staffs to

  • Every truck shadowed end-to-end at least twice per week
  • Every DP observed during serving at least twice per week
  • A 6-day rolling rotation so coverage is even across trucks and DPs
  • One RP can cover roughly 6 DPs per day (3 on truck-follow, 3 independent) — about 36 DP-visits a week, enough for up to ~18 DPs from one kitchen. Beyond that, add field RPs (see the effort calculator, Annexure A)

Hard safety rules

No two-wheeler ride before sunrise — the pre-dawn home-to-kitchen leg is always by cab.

Helmet mandatory; visibility jacket during truck-follow. One RP per truck. RP never solo on a route after 21:00.

ZL may authorise unlimited daily cabs for up to 7 consecutive days; beyond that needs programme-lead approval.

6.4The reconciliation sanity check — know the formula

At weigh-back the RP runs this on at least one container a day:

Formula

returnedKg ≈ filledKg − (headcountServed × 0.400 kg)

If the delta exceeds 10%, log an Exception with a reason — over-portioning, a dropped tray, no-shows, and so on. This is where wastage data is born and where over- or under-serving first shows up.

Portion size

The RP recommends, but only the ZL approves an actual change to the 400g standard.

6.6Escalation — phone calls, not WhatsApp

Most issues are raised as an Exception in food_app. These four require an immediate phone call:

  • Suspected food-poisoning report from any beneficiary — call ZL, programme lead, and Ramani Manager; pause that DP next day pending review
  • Physical altercation, or police involvement — call ZL and programme lead before continuing the day
  • Vehicle accident with injury
  • Anything where a life or the programme's standing is at immediate risk

What improved · what regressed · one decision needed from the ZL · one thing to celebrate at a DP · one risk. No more than five lines.
