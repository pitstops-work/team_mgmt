Module 7

Assessment & Sign-off

All roles, by role Per-role · printable record

No one serves unsupervised until they pass. Assessment is part written (the check-your-understanding questions in each module), part practical (a supervised live shift), and part attitude (observed against the dignity charter). The RP signs each trainee off.

7.1Pass thresholds by role

RoleMust completePractical sign-off
KitchenModules 0–2 + 3One supervised fill-and-seal cycle with zero temperature or seal misses
DriverModules 0–2 + 4One supervised route: correct FILO load, all scans, no red flags
DPModules 0–2 + 5One supervised serve: 400g bowl every plate, queue held, dignity charter clean, site left spotless
RPModules 0–6Full 4-week onboarding (§6.8); independent audit and wastage cut by week 4

7.2Automatic-fail behaviours — retrain before any sign-off

These fail the practical outright

Asking a beneficiary for ID or proof · photographing a face · serving a different portion by appearance · shrinking portions to stretch supply · dispatching or serving food below the temperature floor · skipping a required container scan · handling money in the field.
