Module 8

Quick-Reference Cards · Print & Laminate

All roles One card per role · keep at the site of use

Print one card per role, laminate, and keep at the kitchen, in the cab, and at each DP. These are the SOP's checklists compressed to a single glance.

🖨 Print all four cards
Kitchen Before dispatch
  • Menu posted; raw materials & 1-day buffer present
  • Veg / rice / dal pass reject criteria; oil clean; cold-chain ≤ 5°C
  • Cooks in apron + headcap; handwash stocked; no phones
  • Each container: lining clean, weighed empty, filled to grams + 5%, weighed full → filledKg
  • Core temp ≥ 65°C (3 random); seal + latch; QR → FILLED; photo; labelled
  • Staged reverse-DP order; readiness gate cleared by 04:30
Driver Every route
  • FILO load: last DP first, matches route sheet; each scanned LOADED
  • Phone ≥ 80%, data on, GPS logger ON; dispatch stamped by 05:00
  • Drop: two-person offload, on table / tarp, seal intact, scan DELIVERED, capture droppedKg + photo
  • Drive smooth — no spills; no stops, no passengers, no deviation
  • Collect 45–60 min after drop, service ended; scan PICKED_UP; site swept clean
  • Broken seal / spill / absent staff / fight → photo + Exception NOW
DP Setup to close
  • Both staff in apron + headcap + gloves; site 5m from drains
  • Cap chalked; queue marked; in/out paths don't cross; 400g bowl ready
  • Seal checked intact on receipt; scan acknowledged
  • 400g bowl every plate; one per person; greet & thank; priority for elderly/disabled/pregnant
  • Short on food → announce, close, apologise — NEVER shrink the portion
  • Headcount honest; site swept to zero litter; report incidents
RP Daily spine
  • 01:30 cab to kitchen (never a 2W before sunrise)
  • 02:00 day-start audit · 04:00 fill + temp · 04:30 FILO + brief
  • 05:00 truck-follow (2W, or cab if rain) · 07:30 DP rounds (other truck)
  • 09:30 weigh-back — run: returnedKg ≈ filledKg − (headcount × 0.4kg)
  • 10:45 WhatsApp to ZL: delivered/planned, wastage %, exceptions, anything red
  • Phone-call (not WhatsApp): food-poisoning, altercation, police, accident-with-injury
