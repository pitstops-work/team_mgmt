THE NON-NEGOTIABLES — every role, every day

▸ One plate per person per day. No re-serves. No exceptions.
▸ The full 400g bowl is used to serve — never an estimate, never a different size by appearance.
▸ No ID, ration card, or proof is ever asked. The programme is unconditional.
▸ No photography of beneficiary faces, ever.
▸ Food is served, not dumped. Beneficiaries are guests, not subjects.
▸ No money changes hands at any point in the field chain.

MODULE 4 · 90 MIN
Truck Driver — Load, Route, Drop, Collect
Driver JustDelivery via Sampark

You are the only person who sees the food at every stop. You carry the day from the kitchen to each point and back. Your discipline on time, temperature, and handling is what keeps the chain intact between sites.

  • FILO loading · last-DP-first, scan LOADED, departure by 05:00
  • Route hygiene · no stops, no passengers, smooth driving
  • The drop · two-person offload, on table or tarp, seal intact, scan DELIVERED
  • The collect · 45–60 min after drop, service ended, scan PICKED_UP, leave no trace
  • Red flags · broken seal, spill, absent staff, fight — photo + Exception NOW

MODULE 5 · 120 MIN
Distribution Point Personnel — Setup, Serve, Close
DP 2 staff per DP · identified by Sampark

You are the face of the programme. The beneficiary never meets the cook or the driver — they meet you. Everything earlier in the chain exists so that you can hand over a hot, full, dignified plate.

  • Setup before the truck arrives · cap chalked, queue marked, paths don't cross
  • Receiving the container · seal check, scan acknowledged
  • Serving · 400g bowl every plate, one per person, greet and thank, priority for elderly/disabled/pregnant
  • The shortage rule · announce, close, apologise — NEVER shrink the portion
  • Closing & handover · honest headcount, swept site, packed kit
  • What is never your job · ID, photos, queue jumping, money

MODULE 6 · FULL 4-WEEK ONBOARDING
Resource Person — Operator of Record
RP Our staff · 02:00 to 11:00 shift

The RP is not an auditor. The RP is the operator-of-record. If the kitchen is late or a DP runs out, the RP is accountable for noticing, escalating, and writing down what we learn. Observe, document, escalate — and never handle money, vendor staff hiring, or vendor price.

  • The 9-hour shift · 02:00 day-start audit through 10:45 WhatsApp summary
  • Coverage rules · every truck shadowed end-to-end twice a week, every DP observed serving twice a week
  • Transport & safety floor · never a 2W before sunrise, cab home if tired
  • The reconciliation sanity check · returnedKg ≈ filledKg − (headcount × 0.4kg)
  • The four cross-cutting reviews · procurement, crowd, dignity, wastage
  • Escalation · phone calls (not WhatsApp) for poisoning, altercation, accident, life-risk
  • The 4-week onboarding path · shadow, co-pilot, solo+net, steady state
  • Pitstops-app side · links to launch deck, monthly ops, new DP activation
