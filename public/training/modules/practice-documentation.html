The same three words used everywhere in our operations. The dashboard surfaces every page in one of these states.

THE ONE RULE ABOVE ALL OTHERS

The wiki is downstream of the field. If the page disagrees with what good COs actually do today, the page is wrong, not the COs. The owner's job is to keep the page in lockstep with the practice — not to police the practice into matching the page.

FIVE PLAYERS — KNOW WHICH ONE YOU ARE

Owner, CO, RP, ZL, Curator. Each touches the page differently.

The owner has editorial authority on one page. Everyone else feeds the owner. The curator handles cross-page hygiene only.

Owner

Owns one page. Drafts edits, runs the practice circle (months 1–3), accepts handover. 6-month term. Partner senior staff or our RP.

CO

Partner field staff. Reads pages, flags errors, files gaps, attends circles. The room where practice meets documentation.

RP

Resource Person. Owns pages for our directly-run programmes; attends circles and partner reviews; runs shadow visits across partners.

ZL

Zonal Leader. Watches the dashboard. Notices stale pages, missed circles, gaps that have not moved. Intervenes when the rhythm breaks.

Curator

One per city. Designated, not elected. Cross-page consistency. Weekly gap-queue walk. Weekly stale-page sweep. Triages translation flags. Never edits content without the owner's consent.
