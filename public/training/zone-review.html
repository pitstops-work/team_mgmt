PITSTOP 1 OF 5 · REVIEW · 25 DAYS

RP Data Submissions Review

Why this pitstop

Review all 5 RP(s) data submissions for the monthly cycle. Check for completeness, timeliness, and anomalies before the zone review meeting.

1.
All 5 RP(s) have submitted monthly data
2.
Data completeness verified for each RP
3.
Late submissions logged and reason noted
4.
Key metrics compared against targets (creche coverage, beneficiary counts, activity logs)
5.
Data quality issues flagged to individual RPs
6.
Consolidated zone-level summary prepared
PITSTOP 2 OF 5 · REVIEW · 28 DAYS

Variance & Exception Report

Why this pitstop

Identify underperformance, blockers, and red flags across the zone's 5 RP(s). This feeds directly into the zone review meeting and any escalations.

1.
RPs running behind target identified
2.
Root causes noted for each variance (field, capacity, external)
3.
Escalation-worthy issues flagged to manager
4.
Quick wins and bright spots identified
5.
Variance report document finalised
PITSTOP 3 OF 5 · MEETING · 30 DAYS

Monthly Zone Review Meeting

Why this pitstop

Monthly review with all 5 RP(s). Go through variance report, address blockers, align on next-cycle priorities. Keep to 90 minutes max; follow up async on individual cases.

1.
All 5 RP(s) present (or send written update if absent)
2.
Variance report reviewed with group
3.
Each RP's top blocker acknowledged and owner assigned
4.
Bright spots shared for learning
5.
Next cycle priorities agreed
6.
Action items with owners and due dates documented
7.
Notes circulated to all RPs within 24 hours
PITSTOP 4 OF 5 · REVIEW · 90 DAYS

Quarterly Zone Performance Report

Why this pitstop

Quarterly synthesis across all 5 RP(s). Trends, cumulative outcomes, capacity gaps, and zone-level recommendations to senior leadership.

1.
3-month data compiled for all RPs
2.
Trend analysis completed (month-on-month movement)
3.
Cumulative beneficiary and coverage numbers verified
4.
Capacity gaps and RP development needs documented
5.
Zone-level risks and mitigation plans noted
6.
Recommendations section drafted
7.
Report reviewed with manager before submission
8.
Report submitted to programme leadership
PITSTOP 5 OF 5 · REVIEW · 30 DAYS

Annual Zone Plan

Why this pitstop

Annual planning exercise for the zone. Sets coverage targets, capacity building priorities, partnership goals, and funding requirements for the next 12 months.

1.
Previous year performance reviewed
2.
Coverage gaps across settlements mapped
3.
Input collected from all 5 RP(s) on ground realities
4.
Settlement-level targets set for each RP
5.
Capacity building needs identified and prioritised
6.
Partnership and funding requirements noted
7.
Draft plan shared with senior leadership
8.
Final plan approved and shared with RPs
