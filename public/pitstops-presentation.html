What does Pitstops do?

Five pillars powering your urban programme

🗺️
Geospatial Assessment

Map every settlement polygon in Bangalore & Chennai. See exactly where services exist vs where they're needed — by domain, by zone, by cluster.

🎯
Goal & Task Management

Create goals tied to specific services and geographies. Break them into pitstops with checklists, activities, and due dates.

👥
Team Management

Full hierarchy from RP to Leadership. Every manager sees their team's health — delayed pitstops, overdue activities, engagement levels.

📅
Planning & Scheduling

Weekly planner with Gantt-style views by quarter. Schedule activities, track checklists, plan your field week.

💬
Communication

Threads linked directly to goals, pitstops, and checklist items. Voice logs auto-transcribed and attached. No more scattered WhatsApp updates.

📊
Programme Reporting

8-tab admin dashboard. Track engagement, team health, coverage gaps, and goal pipeline across both cities in real time.

Day-to-Day Work

Activities & Checklists

How field work gets logged and verified

📅 Activities (Pitstop Events)
• Scheduled field visits, meetings, assessments
• Each activity has a date and time
• Status: Scheduled → Done / Rescheduled / Cancelled
• Marking an activity Done auto-closes its linked checklist item
☑️ Checklist Items
• Discrete deliverables inside a pitstop
• Assigned to specific team members
• Can have notes, attachments, voice logs
3 ways to auto-complete:
🎙️ Voice log → transcribed → marked Done
📎 File upload → marked Done automatically
✅ Activity marked Done → linked item closes
🗓️ Activities Calendar
• Full calendar view of all scheduled activities
• Scoped to your team (RP sees own, ZL sees team)
• Red panel shows overdue activities needing action
• Filter by week, reschedule by dragging
🎙️ Voice Log Feature
Record a voice note in any Indian language. Pitstops automatically:
1. Transcribes with Sarvam AI
2. Translates to English via Google
3. Attaches to the checklist item
4. Marks the item as Done

Weekly Planner

Quarter-by-quarter Gantt view for your goals and activities

📅 What the planner shows
• Weeks arranged by financial year quarter (Q1 Apr–Jun · Q2 Jul–Sep · Q3 Oct–Dec · Q4 Jan–Mar)
• Each week row shows: pitstops starting/ending that week, scheduled activities, and plan items you've added manually
• Add anything to a week's plan with a one-click "Add to plan" button
• Overdue items flagged in red
🎯 How to use it for planning
1. Open Setup → Planner
2. Navigate to the current quarter
3. See all active pitstops mapped to weeks by target date
4. Add bespoke plan items for field visits, reviews, or meetings
5. Share your plan view in team standups

Communication

Threads

Contextual conversations linked directly to your goals and work

💬 What is a Thread?

A thread is a discussion that's anchored to a specific piece of work — not floating in WhatsApp. Every thread is linked to:

📁 A Goal — broad programme discussion
📌 A Pitstop — task-specific conversation
☑️ A Checklist item — clarification or evidence
📅 A Scheduled activity — pre/post event notes
📎 Attachments on Threads
• Upload photos, PDFs, documents
• Voice recordings auto-transcribed
• All files stored and searchable
• Uploading to a checklist item auto-closes it
ZLs and PMs see threads from their team members — no more hunting for updates across different messaging apps.
